Simple tools. Real results.

Built around the actual workflow of a small team.

Organizations & workspaces
Create multiple teams and switch between them instantly. Each workspace is completely separate with its own members and projects.
Tasks that actually track
Priorities, due dates, assignments, file attachments, and comments. All updates happen instantly.
Guest access
Invite external collaborators on a per-project basis. They see only what you want them to see.
Activity feed & summaries
See what changed across your whole team or inside a single project. Daily and weekly email digests keep everyone in sync.
AI task breakdown
Describe a goal and get a full task list instantly. A useful shortcut when starting a new project from scratch.
Secure by default
Email verification, Google sign-in, and protection against brute-force attempts. Your team's data stays safe without any setup.
